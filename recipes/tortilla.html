Tortilla

Simple Tortillas (https://www.kingarthurbaking.com/recipes/simple-tortillas-recipe)

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 cups (300g) Unbleached All-Purpose Flour, plus additional as needed
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 cup of any one of the following: 4 tablespoons butter, room temperature (57g), shortening (48g), lard (57g), or vegetable oil (50g)
  • 7/8 to 1 cup (200g to 227g) water, hot, (about 110°F to 120°F)

Steps

  1. Whisk together the flour, baking powder, and salt in a mixing bowl.
  2. Use your fingers to combine the butter/shortening/lard/vegetable oil with the dry ingredients.
  3. Add warm water and knead briefly until a dough ball is formed. Add additional water or flour as required.
  4. Divide the dough into 8 equal pieces, and form each piece into a ball. Flatten each ball slightly, cover and allow to rest for 30 minutes.
  5. Pre-heat a pan over medium-high heat.
  6. For each piece of dough, roll out the dough ball, and fry each side for 30 seconds or until crisp and cooked through.
